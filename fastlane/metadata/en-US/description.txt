MealMate is an AI-powered meal planning app that takes the stress out of deciding what to eat. Get personalized meal plans, discover new recipes, and auto-generate grocery lists.

Features:
• AI-powered weekly meal plan generation
• 1000+ recipes with nutritional information
• Auto-generated grocery lists by store section
• Dietary preference support (vegan, keto, gluten-free, and more)
• Calorie and macro tracking
• Save and organize favorite recipes

Built by the Abraham Oladotun Foundation NFP.
